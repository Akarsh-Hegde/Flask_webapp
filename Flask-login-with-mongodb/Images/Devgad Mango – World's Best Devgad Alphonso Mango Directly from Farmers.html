About Devgad Taluka & Our Organization

Devgad, the heartland of Alphonso mango, is now home to one of the most advanced and largest mango processing factory in the Konkan region of Maharashtra. Devgad Taluka Amba Utpadak Sahakari Sanstha Ltd has started one of the biggest fruit/food processing plant in the Konkan region.

Mango Bonds! 10% Interest Rate In The Form Of Mango
